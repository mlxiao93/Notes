通过不同视口下元素尺寸的比较，确定元素骨架图的尺寸

元素在视口1的绝对尺寸记为 width1, height1, top1, right1, bottom1, left1
元素在视口1的相对视口的百分比尺寸记为 width1_p, height1_p, top1_p, right1_p, bottom1_p, left1_p

元素在视口2的绝对尺寸记为 width2, height2, top2, right2, bottom2, left2
元素在视口2的相对视口的百分比尺寸记为 width2_p, height2_p, top2_p, right2_p, bottom2_p, left2_p


left1 === left2, width1_p === width2_p, right1 === right2

三个值都相等，骨架屏取left、right


left1 === left2, width1 !== width2 && width1_p !== width2_p, right1 === right2

left、right相等，骨架屏取left、right


left1 === left2, width1 === width2, right1 !== right2 && right1_p !== right2_p

left、width相等，骨架屏取left、width




left1 !== left2 && left1_p !== left2_p, width1 === width2, right1 === right2 && right1_p !== right2_p

只有width相等， 骨架屏取left=calc(100vw - ?)
